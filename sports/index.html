1. Chess

15 MARCH | 10:00 AM-1:00 PM
Event Coordinator: Dr. Deepak Gangwar (9718508870)
Fees: Rs. 100/-
Venue: Sports Complex

Strategic battle of the minds. Outwit your opponent to be the Grandmaster.

Competition Format & Rules:
  • Preliminary Round: Conducted under Swiss league or knockout format depending on participant count.
  • Advancement: Qualified players from prelims advance to Quarterfinals and Semifinals based on points scored.
  • Final Round: Top performers compete to determine the overall winners.
  • Supervision: All rounds are conducted under the strict supervision of an arbiter.
  • Tie-Breaks: Official tie-break rules will be applied for recording results in case of draws.
Event Schedule:
  • 10:00 – 11:00 AM: Preliminary Round
  • 11:00 – 11:30 AM: Quarter Finals and Semifinal
  • 11:30 – 12:00 PM: Final Round

2. Slow Cycling Race

14 MARCH | 10:00 AM-1:00 PM
Event Coordinator: Mr. Pankaj Roy (9412294024)
Fees: Rs. 100/-
Venue: Place of Inaugural Ceremony of Fest

The ultimate test of balance. The last person to cross the finish line wins.

Guidelines & Rules:
  • Objective: Complete the course in the maximum possible time.
  • Disqualification: Placing either foot on the ground during the race results in immediate disqualification.
  • Boundaries: Deviation from or contact with the marked track boundaries (side lines) is strictly prohibited.
  • Equipment: Participants must bring their own standard, non-modified bicycles.
  • Prohibitions: Use of geared, electric, motor-assisted, or altered bicycles is prohibited.
  • Conduct: Complete stoppage of the bicycle or any external assistance is not allowed.
Venue Details:
  • Location: Nehru Kendra, MJPRU.
  • Track: Straight track with chalk-marked boundaries.
  • Length: 200–400 meters.

* For more detail regarding safety protocols and official discretion, please see the booklet.
