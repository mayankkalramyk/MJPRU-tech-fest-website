3. Sur Sangam (Singing)

14 March | 2:00 PM–4:00 PM
Event Coordinator: Ms. Shashi Lata (9012323352)
Venue: Panchal Auditorium
Fees: 100

Let your voice touch the hearts in this solo and duet competition.

Competition Rounds:
  • Round 1 (Voice of Choice): Perform a song of your choice (Max 4 mins).
  • Round 2 (Track & Identify): Identify a song from its instrumental track and sing a verse.

  • Equipment: Only handheld wired microphones provided by organizers will be used.
  • Conduct: Lyrics containing obscene or offensive content are strictly prohibited.

* For more detail regarding track submission deadlines and technical sound check slots, please see the booklet.

5. Fusion Dance Challenge

15 March | 10:00 AM–1:00 PM
Event Coordinator: Dr. Reena Pant (9411088903)
Venue: Panchal Auditorium
Fees: 100

Experience the rhythm where classical meets western dance forms.

Competition Rounds:
  • Round 1 (Dual Style Fusion): Perform a single song showcasing at least two distinct styles (e.g., Classical + Hip-Hop).
  • Round 2 (Music Switch Challenge): Teams must instantly adapt their choreography when the music suddenly switches genre mid-performance.
  • Round 3 (Prop Fusion Round): Teams must creatively integrate a prop (dupatta, hat, chair, etc.) into their fusion act.
Rules & Regulations:
  • Audio: Final tracks must be submitted in MP3 format before the deadline; no changes allowed on event day.
  • Props & Costume: Teams must arrange their own props; costumes must be decent and appropriate.
  • Reporting: Teams must report at least 30 minutes before the event starts.
  • Disqualifications: Vulgarity, inappropriate gestures, or use of fire/dangerous materials will lead to immediate disqualification.

* For more detail regarding stage dimensions and specific track submission deadlines, please see the booklet.
