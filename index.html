From the Desk of

Dean Academics

I am pleased to learn that the Faculty of Engineering & Technology, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Rohilkhand University, Bareilly is organizing a grand fest ensuring maximum participation from all stake holders. The fest is aimed to provide the students various opportunities of versatile nature to show their talent in different areas outside the class room teaching and to explore the possibility of applying their expertise in the area of their choice.

The fest is likely to provide students an innovative tool to exhibit their creativity, skills coupled with lot of fun. It will also provide an opportunity to upgrade themselves and to apply it for overall growth and development. It will provide all the students an umbrella to teach themselves and learn from others for overall personality development.

I wish a grand success to this forthcoming fest 26.

From the Desk of

Dean (FET)

It gives me immense pleasure to present Aura 2K26—a vibrant celebration of talent, creativity, and innovation at the Faculty of Engineering & Technology, Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Rohilkhand University. Our Tech—Cultural—Literary Fest is not merely an event; it is a reflection of the dynamic spirit of FET—where ideas are engineered, creativity is celebrated, and leadership is nurtured.

They encourage collaboration, confidence, and the courage to innovate.

Accommodation & Food

Accommodation: Free on-campus hostel accommodation for outstation participants.

Security: Safe and secure hostels located conveniently near fest venues.

Food: Mess facilities available in the hostel on a paid basis.

Note: Inform organizers in advance to avail mess facilities.

Medical: Basic medical assistance available on campus for emergencies.

More Details : please refer to the Hospitality Page of the Booklet.

Register for Accommodation

Open To: All University Students.

Deadline: 24 hours before event start.

Registration Fee: INR 100.00 (Non-refundable).

Exclusive: Free entry in Fashion Show, Battle of Bands & Musical Night for registered participants.

Mandatory: Carry your original College ID card.
